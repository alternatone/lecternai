Option 1

Clean Lines

Minimal styling with subtle separators. Content-first approach with actions that stay out of the way. Uses a thin left border to show reply hierarchy.

Question 1
How does Aquinas's concept of natural law relate to contemporary ethical frameworks?
Sarah Mitchell Dec 10 at 2:34 PM
Aquinas's natural law theory provides a foundation that many contemporary virtue ethicists build upon. The idea that moral truths can be discovered through reason remains influential, though modern interpretations often secularize his theological framework.
James Chen Dec 10 at 3:15 PM
That's a great point about secularization. I'd add that natural law thinking also shows up in human rights discourse, even if not explicitly acknowledged.
Sarah Mitchell Dec 10 at 4:02 PM
Absolutely - the UN Declaration of Human Rights has clear natural law influences.
Michael Torres Dec 11 at 9:22 AM
I think the teleological aspect is what separates Aquinas most clearly from modern ethics. The idea that things have inherent purposes isn't widely accepted in secular philosophy today.
