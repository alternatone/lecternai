Question about illustrations: How do we avoid illustrations that overshadow the biblical text? I've heard sermons where the illustration is more memorable than the Scripture itself.
Dr. Michael Daniels 4 hours ago
Excellent concern, David. The illustration should be a window to the text, not a painting that covers it. Keep illustrations concise and always explicitly connect them back to the biblical truth you're illuminating. If people leave remembering your story but not God's story, the illustration has failed its purpose.
