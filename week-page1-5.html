Intro to the Week

Page 2 of 5

This week we'll explore the art and craft of sermon construction. You'll learn how to develop a clear "big idea," structure your message effectively, and use illustrations that illuminate rather than overshadow Scripture. Through video content and discussion, we'll examine various homiletical approaches and help you develop your own voice as a preacher.

This Week You Will:

  • Identify the "big idea" of a biblical text
  • Compare deductive and inductive sermon structures
  • Learn techniques for crafting compelling introductions and conclusions
  • Explore the role of illustrations in preaching
  • Engage in live discussion with Dr. Daniels and your peers
